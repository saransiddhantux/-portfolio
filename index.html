About me

Being a UI designer
systems thinker
was the reframe.

I started my career designing screens. Somewhere along the way, I realised the most valuable work happened before the first screen was even opened — in the reframing of what problem is actually worth solving.

Today I work as a Lead Product Designer at Piramal Finance, where I've shaped AI-assisted workflows, digital lending journeys, and internal tooling at scale. My work sits at the intersection of product strategy, interaction design, and systems thinking.

I'm drawn to problems where the surface ask is one thing, and the real fix is another. That's where design can actually move the needle.

What I believe in

Values I work by.

The way I work is shaped as much by values as by craft. These are the principles I return to when things get ambiguous.

01
Understand the human before the interface.

I start with motivations and fears, not wireframes. Solving the wrong problem beautifully is still failure.

02
Every screen is a decision moment.

I design for the judgment happening inside the user's head — not just the interaction happening on the screen.

03
Measure impact, not output.

Screens shipped is not a metric. I anchor my work in what actually changed — conversion, trust, retention, behavior.

04
Stay curious, stay unfinished.

The best insights come from staying open. My thinking evolves with every problem, not just every project.

When I'm not designing

Mountains, scuba diving, skydiving, good people, and moments worth remembering.
